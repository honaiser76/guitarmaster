Guitar Master

Learn guitar theory in a visual, interactive way

GuitarMaster is a capstone project concept that explores how digital media can improve music education for beginner and intermediate guitarists. The platform is designed to make music theory easier to understand by connecting scales, chords, intervals, and harmony directly to the guitar fretboard through interactive tools and AI-supported learning.

Signal Intensity

Where Music Theory
Meets Digital Learning

Many guitar learners struggle with theory because it often feels abstract and disconnected from real playing. GuitarMaster addresses this challenge by presenting music concepts in a more visual, practical, and engaging way.

The project combines user-centered design, digital learning tools, and emerging technology to support deeper understanding and more accessible music education.
