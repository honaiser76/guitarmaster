At GuitarMaster, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may share through this website. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it may be used, and how visitors can interact with the site safely.

01 About This Website

GuitarMaster is a digital media capstone project website created to present a concept for an interactive guitar learning platform. The website is intended for educational and informational purposes. At this stage, GuitarMaster is a project concept and does not operate as a commercial service, subscription platform, or fully functional mobile application.

02 Information We May Collect

Depending on how the website is built and hosted, limited information may be collected automatically, such as:

  • check_circle Browser and device specifications
  • check_circle Pages visited and interaction logs
  • check_circle Approximate location based on IP address

If the website includes a contact form or similar feature, visitors may also choose to provide personal information such as: name, email address, and message content.

03 How Information Is Used

Any information collected through the website may be used to improve the website’s design and user experience, understand how visitors interact with the project, respond to inquiries, and support academic evaluation. Personal information will not be sold or used for unrelated marketing purposes.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use basic analytics to understand general visitor activity. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to external learning resources. GuitarMaster is not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party websites.

06 Data Security

Reasonable efforts are made to keep the website secure. However, no online platform can guarantee complete security. Visitors should avoid submitting sensitive personal information through the site.

07 Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for general educational audiences and is not specifically directed to children under 13. The site does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

08 Educational Purpose

Because GuitarMaster is a student capstone project, some features described on the website may be conceptual rather than fully active. Any references to AI-supported learning, personalization, or interactive tools are presented as part of the project design and research focus.

09 Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the website changes in scope, features, or hosting platform. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the GuitarMaster project, you may contact the website creator through the available contact method on the site.
